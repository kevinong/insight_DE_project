Hi everyone, my name is Kevin and my project is GoodReviews, which recommend reviewers for products. 

The motivation is simple: to get good reviews and thus increase revenue. If there are multiple product options available, most online buyers would rely heavily on user reviews to make decisions on whether to purchuse the item or not. So, if the business owner can find quality reviewers for their products and therefore helpful reviews, the sales might go up.
